About WebMuseum

This project is dedicated to preserving the look and feel of the early 2000’s Internet.
Flash banners, hit counters, guestbooks – all in one place.
Step back in time and explore what the web used to look like!

Remember when websites had music, blinking gifs and counters?
WebMuseum brings those memories back – and keeps them alive for the next generations.
